# 🚀 Fertility Health & Pregnancy Success Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Infertility and reproductive health challenges affect millions of couples worldwide. Understanding the factors that influence pregnancy success can help healthcare professionals make better treatment decisions and improve patient outcomes.

This end-to-end Data Analytics project explores fertility health data to identify key drivers of pregnancy success and uncover actionable healthcare insights through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), interactive Power BI dashboards, and predictive analytics.

The project combines Python, Data Visualization, Business Intelligence, and Machine Learning techniques to transform raw healthcare data into meaningful insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to analyze fertility-related factors and determine how demographic, lifestyle, medical, and treatment variables impact pregnancy outcomes.

### Key Questions Addressed

- How does age influence pregnancy success?
- What impact does PCOS have on fertility outcomes?
- Does stress level affect pregnancy success rates?
- How do lifestyle factors such as smoking and alcohol consumption impact fertility?
- Which treatment methods show higher success rates?
- Can pregnancy outcomes be predicted using historical fertility data?

# 📈 Key Findings

## 🏆 Overall Pregnancy Success Rate

- Pregnancy Success Rate: **72.75%**
- Successful Pregnancies: **582**
- Failed Outcomes: **218**

---

## 👩 Female Age Impact

Female age emerged as one of the strongest predictors of pregnancy success.

### Observation

- Women under 30 achieved the highest success rates.
- Success rates gradually declined with increasing age.

### Business Insight

Early fertility assessment and intervention may improve outcomes for higher age groups.

---

## 🩺 PCOS Analysis

Women diagnosed with PCOS demonstrated lower pregnancy success rates compared to non-PCOS patients.

### Business Insight

PCOS should be considered a high-priority risk factor when designing fertility treatment plans.

---

## 😟 Stress Level Impact

Stress levels showed a noticeable association with pregnancy outcomes.

### Observation

- Low Stress → Higher Success Rate
- High Stress → Lower Success Rate

### Business Insight

Mental wellness and stress-management programs may positively contribute to fertility outcomes.

---

## 🚬 Lifestyle Factors

Smoking and alcohol consumption negatively impacted fertility outcomes.

### Business Insight

Lifestyle counseling can play a significant role in improving reproductive health.

---

## 🧬 Sperm Health Analysis

Higher sperm count and motility were associated with increased pregnancy success rates.

### Business Insight

Male fertility indicators remain critical variables in fertility treatment planning.

---

## 💉 Treatment Effectiveness

Different fertility treatments produced varying success rates.

### Observation

IVF treatments demonstrated stronger pregnancy success compared to alternative treatment approaches.
